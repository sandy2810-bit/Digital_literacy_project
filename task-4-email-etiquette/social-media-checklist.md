# Social Media Do's and Don'ts checklist

## ✔️ Do's 

- Maintain a respectful and positive tone in your posts
- Think before posting or sharing content
- Protect your privacy by adjusting account settings
- Verify information before sharing it
- Use social media to build skills and showcase achievements

## ❌ Don'ts 

- Don’t share personal or sensitive information publicly
- Don’t engage in online arguments or offensive comments
- Don’t spread misinformation or fake news
- Don’t post inappropriate or unprofessional content
- Don’t waste excessive time on social media
