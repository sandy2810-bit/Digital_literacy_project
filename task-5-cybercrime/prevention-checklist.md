# 🛡️ Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

## ✅ Do’s for Staying Safe Online

- Use strong, unique passwords for each account (mix of letters, numbers, symbols)
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on important accounts
- Never share OTPs, PINs, or banking details with anyone
- Always verify unknown links, emails, or messages before clicking
- Keep your apps, browser, and antivirus updated regularly
- Use only trusted Wi-Fi networks; avoid public Wi-Fi for transactions
- Check app permissions before installing new apps

## 💰 UPI / Financial Safety Tips

- Never approve unknown UPI collect requests or scan random QR codes
- Double-check the receiver’s name before sending money
- Do not trust calls/messages claiming to be from bank officials without verification
## 🚫 Avoid These
- Don’t download apps from unknown sources
- Don’t overshare personal information on social media
- Don’t reuse the same password across multiple platforms
