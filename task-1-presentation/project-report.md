# Project Report

I created my digital literacy infographic using Canva, a user-friendly design tool that offers a wide variety of templates and visual elements. The design presents key aspects of digital literacy in a simple and visually engaging format. It includes sections on the meaning of digital literacy, useful digital tools for students, safe internet practices, professional online presence, and email etiquette. Each section is supported with icons and short bullet points to make the content easy to understand at a glance.

The infographic uses a clean layout with balanced colors and readable fonts to ensure clarity and visual appeal. I focused on making the information concise so that students can quickly grasp the important concepts without feeling overwhelmed.

One challenge I faced while creating the design was selecting the right layout and organizing the content effectively within a single page. However, this process helped me understand the importance of visual hierarchy and simplicity in communication. Overall, this task was interesting as it improved my design skills and awareness of digital responsibility.
